Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

We focus on building a strong foundation for learning through play, exploration, and sensory activities. Children will develop essential skills like language, literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional learning.

Elementary School

We provide a well-rounded education that covers core subjects like math, science, language arts, social studies, and physical education. We also offer enrichment programs in music, art, and technology.

Middle (10-16) Years

We offer a challenging and rigorous curriculum that prepares students for high school and beyond. We provide opportunities for advanced learning and exploration in areas like STEM, humanities, and the arts.
